Why Us?

At Smart Auto Financial, we specialize in helping clients build, recover, and maintain their credit. We understand the financial and emotional impact that our clients experience when their scores are not what lenders want to see. This is a common problem faced by many borrowers today: 1 in 5 Canadians have a score of 750 or below. But while the banks and dealerships would have borrowers believe that they are out of options when they are turned away:

  • our Senior Credit Analyst, George, has proven otherwise. With over 20 years of experience in the lending industry—16 of which have been spent working directly with borrowers in the credit department at Big 5 Canadian Banks.
  • Alan & Vannessa has helped many of clients obtain secured and unsecured loans, as well as other credit and financing products, even in the face of double bankruptcy.
    “We’ve all made mistakes in the past, but the only thing that keeps us from overcoming them is the lack of information—information that is not made widely available to the average consumer. We’re here to bridge the gap between what the lenders know and what the borrower knows.”
  • George Wong While many of us are told to get a credit card as a necessity, not many have had access to a credit expert or credit analyst to educate them on how to manage it, and most importantly, how to build and maintain a high credit score.

That is why we offer a free consultation with our Senior Credit Analyst, who can help you answer a wide variety of lending questions at no cost, including:

  • What credit card should I get? When should I use it and pay it off?
  • What affects my credit score and how can I increase it?
  • Who can help me navigate the lending system?
  • How can I better manage my debt?
  • Who can I go to when other mainstream lenders have turned me down?
  • What do I need to do if I want to achieve a certain goal in the near future, but my credit score is preventing me from doing so?

Our goal at Smart Auto Financial is to help our clients get their life back on track and to prove to them that a single mistake in their past does not have to define their future. We are committed to giving our clients personalized advice to help them meet their financial goals and aspirations, no matter their current situation. That is why we’ve ingrained customer service into our Code of Ethics: we truly believe in fighting for our client’s interests and making you our top priority in everything we do. With our advanced tools, lending expertise, and inside industry knowledge, we offer a risk-free, reciprocal, and transparent process that is designed to set our clients on the path to success.
